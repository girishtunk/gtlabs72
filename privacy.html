Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 13, 2026

Overview

Smart Notifier automates phone sound and notification modes using your rules and selected triggers. We designed the app to process data on your device whenever possible.

Data We Process

  • Automation rules, profiles, and settings you configure in the app
  • Schedule data (day/time triggers) used to run your rules
  • Optional trigger data you enable, such as Wi-Fi SSID, Bluetooth device name, calendar event details, activity state, and geofence/location context

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration data (no in-app account, email, or phone signup)
  • No in-app payments, subscriptions, or payment data
  • No sale of personal information

How Data Is Used

  • Apply sound/notification profile automation you configure
  • Run trigger checks needed for background automation
  • Provide user-requested support when you contact us

Permissions and Purpose

  • Notification and Do Not Disturb access: required to change notification/sound behavior
  • Location (including background location, if enabled): required for geofence/location-based triggers
  • Nearby Wi-Fi and Bluetooth access: required for network/device-based triggers
  • Calendar access: required for calendar event-based triggers
  • Physical activity recognition: required for activity-based triggers

Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell your data. We do not share trigger data with third parties except when required by law.

Retention and Deletion

Automation settings are stored locally on your device until you change, reset, or uninstall the app. Since Smart Notifier does not provide user accounts, there is no account deletion workflow.

Children

Smart Notifier is for a general audience and is not directed to children.
