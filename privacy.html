Privacy Policy

Overview

GTLabs72 respects your privacy. This policy explains how Smart Notifier and other GTLabs72 products handle data.

How We Use Information

  • Enable core app functionality
  • Maintain reliability and performance
  • Provide support when you contact us

Permissions

Smart Notifier may request access to notifications, Do Not Disturb, and optional location services to apply your automation rules.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data if required by law or to provide essential services.

Data Retention

App settings are stored locally by default. Any optional diagnostics are retained only as long as needed to improve performance.
