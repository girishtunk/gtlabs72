Smart Notifier

Automatic sound mode updates that respect your schedule

Smart Notifier keeps your phone in the right mode without constant toggling. Configure once, and it quietly handles the rest.

App Overview

Smart Notifier updates your sound mode based on your settings and triggers, so you never miss important moments or alerts.

  • Silent during meetings
  • Vibrate during commute
  • Ring after hours

Key Features

  • Rule-based profiles
  • Schedule-based automation
  • Location and time triggers
  • Quick override widget
  • Battery-friendly behavior

How It Works

  1. Create sound profiles
  2. Attach triggers (time, location, calendar)
  3. Smart Notifier switches modes automatically

Platform & Availability

  • Category: Utilities
  • Platforms: Android, iOS (planned)
  • Release status: Coming soon

Permissions & Data Use

Smart Notifier only uses the permissions needed to switch sound modes and apply your rules.

  • Notification access (to apply rules)
  • Do Not Disturb access (to change modes)
  • Location (optional, for location rules)

Privacy Summary

We minimize data collection and do not sell personal information.

  • No third-party data sale
  • Settings stored locally by default
  • Transparent permissions

FAQ

Will Smart Notifier drain battery?

No. It is designed to run efficiently and only activates when needed.

Does it work offline?

Yes. All rules and automation can run without a network connection.

Can I override a rule?

Yes. Use the quick override widget to change modes instantly.

Where is my data stored?

Settings are stored locally by default.
